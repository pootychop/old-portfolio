Edward Jones

  • Graphic Design

Edward Jones is launching an effort to recruit more financial advisors in Canada. They defined a few perks they would like to highlight.

The user will click on a facebook ad and get to a landing page where they can read more about Edward Jones. We created a separate landing page per ad, so that we could match the header to the ad. Click through may feel more consistent, less spammy this way.
