Cintas

  • Graphic Design

With The Arland Group, I created many assets for Cintas recruitment efforts. As a spin-off from their marketing brand, we created the look and feel for their employment brand. Cintas uniforms get people ready for the workday. Their employees have to be ready themselves. We always emphasize the fact that they're hiring, as this is the main thing we want to communicate. We've echoed the Cintas slogan with an "I'm ready" patterned background.
