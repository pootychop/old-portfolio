Hangar By Night

  • Branding
  • Interactive Design
  • UX

Hangar by Night will be an online marketplace to connect pilots with hangar owners. Pilots will have more destinations where they can park their planes out of the elements.

To kick this thing off, we decided to start with a simple splash page. We are collecting contact information of those interested in participating. We will then survey our audience to dial in the application, making sure we meet the needs of our target group.
