350.org

  • Branding
  • Print Design
  • Web Design
  • Front-End Development

The Organization:

350.org is a climate justice organization that I had the opportunity to work with when their designer took a sabbatical. It is a global organization with many movements and campaigns working under the umbrella or 350.org. This was such a great experience, getting to collaborate with people all over the globe.

The Challenge:

We had to visually differentiate campaigns while following the 350.org brand standards.

The Design:

I was able to create a number of different looks for each campaign, and organizational spin-off, while using the same color pallet and fonts.
