Turner Roofing

  • Branding
  • Web Design

The Challenge:

Turner Roofing had a tech savvy relative who built them a big site with alllll the bells and whistles. There was no design congruity. There were so many videos and animations that it would immediately make your computer fan go into overdrive. It was difficult to find the information you needed. The brand colors were maroon and gray, setting a very somber tone.

The Solution:

I redesigned the whole site. I introduced some brighter colors to the brand to help links and buttons stand out. I reorganized and edited down all the information for a more intuitive flow.
