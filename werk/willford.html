Willford Strategies

  • Branding

The Task:

Jenny Willford is a consultant for women running for office. She needed a logo. She wanted it to be colorful, vibrant, fresh and modern. She's drawn to geometric shapes and bright colors.

The Solution:

I pitched a few initial ideas to Jenny, including pink donkeys, a color overlay W, and witchy geometry. We liked the color overlay and sacred geometry. I ended up rotating the W around into a geometric form. We picked some fun colors inspired from some street art.
