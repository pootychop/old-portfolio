Radius

  • Branding
  • Interactive Design

Radius is directory of activities for kids. The features will be determined by the results of our surveys and testing. We have created these mockups to start the conversation with parents, to find out what is important to them.

Our mission is to create an application that makes it easier to get you kids involved in activities. Find activities close to home, that occur on the days you don't have to work late.

We will begin testing with the familiar UI patterns established in Yelp, then make adjustments and improvements to meet parents' needs.
