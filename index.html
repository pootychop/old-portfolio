Hello.
I'm Julianna Beckert

Graphic Designer

As a sculpture major in college, I learned speak my audience's language, and take harsh criticism. As a carpenter doing home renovations, I learned to listen to my customer's needs and design carefully planned solutions.

In the 6 years I've been designing, I've worked my way from Intern to Creative Director. I have a lot of passion for finding great solutions to the worlds design problems. Design is successful when integrity, functionality and aesthetics are synchronous, working together with equal weight and importance.
